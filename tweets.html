SOCCER

Soccer is a game that involves 11 players that play using one ball with the aim of scoring higher goals on the
opponent's goalpost.There are defenders, midfielders and attackers. Other terms you will probably hear is offensive,
offside,goalkick,lastman,winger,leftback,rightback, centerback and many more
